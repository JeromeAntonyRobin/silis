# Maintainers

Silis is developed and maintained by a small core team with defined responsibilities.
Each maintainer contributes independently while respecting the shared vision of the project.

---

## Jerome Antony Robin  
**Lead & Vision Steward**

Focus:
- Project architecture and long-term direction  
- UX philosophy and workflow design  
- Tool integration (RTL → GDSII flow)  
- Licensing, governance, and attribution  
- Public representation of the project  

Role:
Protects the identity, philosophy, and coherence of Silis.

---

- Build system and packaging  
- Dependency handling and deployment flow

---

## Venkat

---

## Adhil  
**Cloud & Deployment Engineer**

Focus:

- Web and hosting infrastructure  
- Accessibility for students without hardware

---

## Shared Responsibility

All maintainers share responsibility for:
- Code quality  
- Documentation  
- Respecting project philosophy  
- Collaboration and communication  

---

Silis is not governed by a single developer, but guided by a unified vision.
Technical decisions are shared.  
Philosophical, UX, and public-facing decisions are stewarded by the Lead.
